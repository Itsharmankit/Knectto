About Knectto

We started because great marketing was too hard to find.

In 2026, we built the agency we always wished existed — honest, focused, and obsessed with delivering results that actually move your business forward.

Our Story

Born out of frustration. Built on purpose.

Knectto was founded in 2026 by a team of marketers, designers, engineers, and AI strategists who were tired of seeing brands settle for agencies that talked big and delivered little. We saw websites shipped without strategy, AI used as a buzzword instead of real automation, marketing budgets wasted on unmeasured campaigns, and clients left without clear analytics or accountability.

So we built something different. A tight, senior team where every client gets real attention — not passed off to a junior after the pitch. We cap our client roster intentionally. Fewer clients, deeper focus, better results.

Today, Knectto works with startups, D2C brands, and B2B companies across India who want marketing that moves fast, thinks strategically, and delivers growth they can measure.

Our Mission

To help ambitious brands grow faster through honest strategy, bold creativity, and marketing that actually works.
